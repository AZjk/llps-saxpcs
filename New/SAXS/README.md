### SAXS analysis for H06

This is the folder that plots the SAXS results on ~100 mg/mL H06 at 12-ID-B. 2021-1 samples were in capillaries and 2021-3 samples were in aluminum cells. 

Both analysis were done by stitching the SA and WA results. The WA intensity has a coefficient that was empirically determined. Currently, 2021-3 data set was missing a few temperature points in the middle of the sweep, and the background subtraction wasn't done.
